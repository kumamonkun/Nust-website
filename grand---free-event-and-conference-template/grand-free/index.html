ABOUT US

The NUST Annual Programming Competition is a prestigious event hosted by the Faculty of Computing and Informatics at the Namibia University of Science and Technology (NUST). Established to inspire critical thinking and enhance problem-solving skills, this competition invites learners from high schools and tertiary institutions across Namibia. Through various coding challenges, participants engage in friendly competition, sharpen their programming expertise, and network with peers and professionals. Our goal is to foster a love for technology, showcase emerging talent, and create an environment that encourages innovation and collaboration within the computing field."

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the NUST Annual Programming Competition?
What is the NUST Annual Programming Competition? The NUST Annual Programming Competition is an event organized by the Faculty of Computing and Informatics at the Namibia University of Science and Technology (NUST). It aims to foster critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and an interest in computing among high school and tertiary students.
Who can participate in the competition?
the competition is open to students from high schools and tertiary institutions. High school students must attend in person at the NUST campus in Windhoek, while tertiary students can participate remotely or in person.
When is the competition taking place?
The competition for the tertiary category will run from October 29th to November 1st, 2024, and the high school competition will be held on November 2nd, 2024. The awards ceremony for both categories is on November 3rd, 2024.
How do I register for the competition?
You can register by clicking the “Register Now” link on the homepage and filling out the registration form. Be sure to complete all required fields and provide accurate information.
What are the Requirements for the competition?
You’ll need to provide personal information (name, email, phone number), your institution details, preferred programming language, IDE, team members, and mentor details if applicable. High school and tertiary categories have different requirements, so check the registration form for details
Can I register individually, or do I need to form a team?
Participation is only allowed through teams. Each team must have a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 6 members. All team members must come from the same institution.
Can I change my team members after registering?
No, changes to team members are not allowed after the competition entry has been submitted.
Do high school students need to be physically present at NUST?
Yes, high school teams are required to attend the competition physically at the NUST campus in Windhoek on November 2nd, 2024.
